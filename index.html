Exploring Diani

Daini Beach Kenya is often referred to as a collective term for a group of interconnected beaches located south of Mombasa that make up Diani Beach. Two of the most popular beaches in Kenya are Diani Beach and Galu Beach, which each stretch for 15 kilometers over powdery white sand. Additionally, there are beaches such as Tiwi, Diani, Galu, Kikondo, and Chale that are also worth visiting on a Kenya Safari Tour to Diani Beach.
